HOW TO PLAY?

The number of rounds to be played is up to the players


1) The game can be played with at least two players and a maximum of your choice.

2) The player making the news channel frame is given a prompt which is the headline.

3) The player then uploads a video or a photo which they think would make everyone else laugh.

4) Every other player gives the news channel a rating of 1 to 5, these ratings are then added

5) The player with the highest amount of points in a given set of rounds wins the game
