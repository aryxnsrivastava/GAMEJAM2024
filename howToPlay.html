How To Play?

The number of rounds to be played is upto the players


1) The game can be played with atleast 2 players and a maximum of 7 players.

2) The player making the news channel frame is given a prompt which is the headline.

3) The player then uploads a video or a photo which they think would make everyone else laugh.

4) Every other player gives the news channel from a rating of 1 to 5, these ratings are then added

5) The player with highest amount of points in a given set of rounds wins the game
